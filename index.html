About

Dr. Jhoselle Tus is a distinguished Filipino scholar, a prolific researcher, and a dedicated educator. He holds a Doctor of Philosophy in Educational Psychology. Dr. Tus serves as the Editor-in-Chief of Psychology and Education: A Multidisciplinary Journal, affiliated with Adnan Menderes University in Turkey. With over 190 published works, his research is widely cited and accessible on global academic platforms.
